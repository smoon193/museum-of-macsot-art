About
Macsot came into this world as a simple object with a simple purpose. A badminton shuttlecock - a foam ball, a woven plastic skirt, a tuft of crunchy orange hair for some reason - meant to be whacked back and forth over a net. But with a fateful flick of a ballpoint pen, that humble shuttlecock became so much more. An enigmatically cheerful expression and a bewildering density of eyelashes allowed Macsot's charming personality to shine through, and thus, a star was born.

Macsot was warmly embraced as the ACS team mascot (or mACSot, if you will): a figure to rally around, a beloved symbol of unity, a beacon of light during difficult sprints. Macsot's Mona Lisa smile enchanted and mystified her/his/their teammates, inspiring memes, art, custom emojis, gender discourse... Macsot may never have submitted a pull request, but it is a cosmic truth that ACS would not be the same without their/his/her profound contributions.
